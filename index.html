My Favorite Hobby:
Art

My biggest hobby is art! Art, in general, is like the expression of a creative gesture and imagination. Art has many styles of creativity, such as painting, music, dance, literature, and photography.
I love art because I can express emotions like beauty, sadness, and happiness with lots of different gestures. I love dancing, painting, and music. I love sharing my art with many people. It makes me feel like I can be the boss of my own creations and feel proud of them. Yes, there are many times where I am not pleased with some creations, but through trial and error, I improve and become better at what I am doing.
I love dancing. I have been studying ballet for almost 9 years. Ballet is a very classical dance and is very therapeutic. Some people say dancing is very easy. No, it is not at all! And that is partially why I like it. I like challenges because once I complete a challenge that proves to me that I am able to do beyond what I am capable to do. It makes me feel accomplished. My favorite ballerina is Mariah Korea. Another style of dance I really like is hip hop, jazz, modern, and musical theatre. I also really like it because it can keep me active throughout the day. Dance is through the physical movements of the body.

I love painting! I love the way I can express anything through different brushstrokes and create something with that. I love painting with gouache and a little bit of acrylic. I have never tried oil painting but maybe sometime in the future. I also really like digital art. I have been studying parts of the human body, such as hands, chest, face, legs, and feet. I don’t really have a favorite artist, but I like Vincent Van Goh and Michaelangelo’s creations.

I love music! I can never live without music, to be honest. It is the best thing ever to do something with! I love listening to music when I read, do homework, dance, cook, paint, and everything else I do! I have been playing piano since I was 4 and I am pretty good at it. I am pretty advanced in it too! I like learning many classical and romantic pieces on the piano. I also have been playing the guitar and ukulele. My favorite style of music I like to listen to is Korean Pop or Korean Rap. My favorite artist these days have been Woo or DPR LIVE or Hwa Sa.
